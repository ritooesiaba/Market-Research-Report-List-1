FOSBs facilitate efficient transport and storage of wafers, offering easy access while maintaining protection. In contrast, FOUPs are designed for automated production environments, providing a sealed, controlled atmosphere for wafers, minimizing contamination risks during handling. Both types are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the safe management of delicate wafers throughout the production and logistics processes.</p></p>
