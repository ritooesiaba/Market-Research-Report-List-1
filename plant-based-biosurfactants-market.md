<p><strong>The Plant Based Biosurfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sophorolipid</li><li>Rhamnolipid</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plant-based biosurfactants market is primarily categorized into three types: sophorolipids, rhamnolipids, and others. Sophorolipids are glycolipids derived from yeast fermentation, known for their eco-friendly properties and effectiveness in emulsifying and wetting agents. Rhamnolipids, produced by bacteria, possess antimicrobial attributes and are utilized in cleaning and agricultural applications. The "others" category includes various biosurfactants such as mannosylerythritol lipids and fatty acid derivatives, which offer diverse functionalities across industries including personal care, agriculture, and food.</p></p>
