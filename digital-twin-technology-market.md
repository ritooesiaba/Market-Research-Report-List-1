<p><ul><li>Parts Twin</li><li>Product Twin</li><li>Process Twin</li><li>System Twin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Twin Technology encompasses various market types: </p><p>1. **Parts Twin** focuses on individual components, providing real-time data and performance insights.</p><p>2. **Product Twin** represents entire products, simulating their functioning and lifecycle for optimization.</p><p>3. **Process Twin** illustrates manufacturing or operational workflows, enhancing efficiency and productivity.</p><p>4. **System Twin** integrates multiple products and processes, allowing holistic monitoring and management of complex systems. Each type serves specific use cases, facilitating decision-making, predictive maintenance, and innovation.</p></p>
