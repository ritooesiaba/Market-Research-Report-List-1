Strong acid resins are typically used for various applications requiring high capacity cation exchange, while weak acid resins are suitable for softening and demineralization processes. Strong base resins are effective for anion exchange in water treatment and purification, whereas weak base resins are used in applications where moderate anion removal is needed.
