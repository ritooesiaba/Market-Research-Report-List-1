<p><p>The tailless excavator market consists of two main types: crawler tailless excavators and wheeled tailless excavators. In contrast, wheeled tailless excavators are mounted on wheels, providing greater mobility and speed on paved surfaces, which suits urban environments and less demanding terrains.
