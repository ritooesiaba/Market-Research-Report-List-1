<p><p>The Smart Pet Collar market primarily features two types: GPS-based and radio-based systems. GPS-based collars utilize satellite technology to track a pet's location in real-time, offering precise positioning and geofencing capabilities, making them ideal for outdoor adventures. On the other hand, radio-based collars operate using short-range radio frequencies, providing reliable tracking in localized environments, such as around the house or yard.
