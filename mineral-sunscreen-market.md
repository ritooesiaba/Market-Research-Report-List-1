<p><p>The mineral sunscreen market primarily consists of two types: titanium dioxide and zinc oxide sunscreens. Titanium dioxide sunscreens provide broad-spectrum UV protection and are often favored for their lightweight feel and less whitening effect on the skin. Both types are environmentally friendly and are gaining popularity due to their non-toxic ingredients and physical blocking properties, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
