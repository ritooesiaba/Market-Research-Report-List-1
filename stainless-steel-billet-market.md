<p><p>The stainless steel billet market encompasses two primary types: round billets and square billets. Round billets are cylindrical in shape, commonly used for manufacturing pipes, tubes, and high-strength components due to their uniform distribution of strength. In contrast, square billets have a flat, angular design, suitable for producing bars, plates, and structural materials. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and aerospace, driven by the demand for durable, corrosion-resistant materials in various applications.</p></p>
