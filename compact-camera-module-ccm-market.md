<p><p>Compact Camera Modules (CCM) are essential components in devices like smartphones and tablets, enabling high-quality imaging capabilities. The market for CCM primarily consists of two sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for superior image quality and low noise, making them suitable for professional applications. In contrast, CMOS sensors are more power-efficient, cost-effective, and allow for faster processing speeds, making them popular in consumer electronics. Both technologies contribute significantly to the evolving imaging landscape.</p></p>
