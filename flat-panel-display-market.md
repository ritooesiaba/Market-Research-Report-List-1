<p><ul><li>Organic Light Emitting Diode Display(OLED)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Display(LCD)</li><li>Plasma Display(PDP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat panel display market includes several key technologies. Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) displays offer vibrant colors and deep blacks, utilizing self-emitting pixels for superior contrast. Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) panels use liquid crystals and a backlight, popular for their affordability and energy efficiency, though usually not as vibrant as OLEDs. Plasma Display Panels (PDP) provide excellent color saturation and wide viewing angles, often favored for larger screens, though they are less common today due to advancements in competing technologies.</p></p>
