<p><ul><li>Single-Point Injection</li><li>Continuous Injection</li><li>Central Port Injection</li><li>Multipoint Fuel Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Swirl Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) System market includes various types, each with distinct characteristics. Single-Point Injection utilizes one injector for multiple engine cylinders, optimizing fuel distribution. Continuous Injection maintains a constant fuel flow, ideal for certain performance applications. Central Port Injection delivers fuel to the intake manifold, enhancing efficiency. Multipoint Fuel Injection features individual injectors for each cylinder, improving performance. Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better efficiency. Swirl Injection enhances mixing of air and fuel, optimizing combustion.</p></p>
