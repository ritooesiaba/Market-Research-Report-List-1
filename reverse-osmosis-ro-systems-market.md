<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems market can be categorized into single-stage and double-stage systems. Single-stage RO systems utilize one membrane unit to remove impurities from water, offering simplicity and efficiency for residential and light commercial applications. In contrast, double-stage RO systems employ two membrane units in series, enhancing purification by achieving a higher rejection rate of contaminants, making them suitable for industrial and specialized applications where water quality is critical. Both systems contribute significantly to clean water solutions.</p></p>
