<p><p>The Fibre-Optic Gyroscope (FOG) market is categorized into Single-Axis and Multi-Axis types. Single-Axis FOGs measure rotation around a single axis, making them ideal for applications requiring simple angular measurements, such as automotive systems or drone stabilization. In contrast, Multi-Axis FOGs can track rotational movements across multiple axes, offering more complex motion data. These are primarily used in aerospace, navigation systems, and robotics, where precise orientation information is crucial for performance and safety.</p></p>
