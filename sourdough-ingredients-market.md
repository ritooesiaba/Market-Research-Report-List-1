<p><strong>The Sourdough Ingredients Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Sourdough</li><li>Devitalized Sourdough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sourdough ingredients market is primarily divided into two types: active sourdough and devitalized sourdough. Active sourdough contains live cultures and is used in baking to enhance flavor and texture. It provides natural leavening and contributes to the unique sour taste. Devitalized sourdough, on the other hand, undergoes processes that deactivate the cultures, making it shelf-stable while retaining flavor properties. This type is often used in commercial products for consistency and convenience, catering to varied consumer and industrial needs.</p></p>
