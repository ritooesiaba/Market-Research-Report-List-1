<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Membrane Filtration Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration (MF)</li><li>Ultrafiltration (UF)</li><li>Nanofiltration (NF)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane filtration systems encompass various technologies, each suited for specific applications. Microfiltration (MF) is designed to remove larger particles and some bacteria, often used in water treatment. Ultrafiltration (UF) targets smaller molecules and colloids, making it effective for wastewater and food processing. Nanofiltration (NF) bridges the gap between UF and reverse osmosis (RO), removing divalent ions and organic compounds. Reverse osmosis (RO) is the most refined, eliminating nearly all dissolved salts and impurities, commonly applied in desalination and purified water production.</p></p>
