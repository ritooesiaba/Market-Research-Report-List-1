<p><p>The racing aluminum wheels market is primarily divided into two types: casting aluminum wheels and forging aluminum wheels. Casting aluminum wheels are made by pouring molten aluminum into molds, resulting in a cost-effective option suitable for many applications. In contrast, forging aluminum wheels are produced by shaping solid aluminum under high pressure, providing superior strength and lightweight properties, ideal for high-performance racing scenarios. Each type caters to different performance needs, with forged wheels typically favored for their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
