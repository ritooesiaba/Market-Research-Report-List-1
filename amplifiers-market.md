<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Amplifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li><li>Class-G&H</li><li>Class-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amplifiers are categorized into various classes based on efficiency and performance characteristics. Class-A amplifiers deliver high linearity but are less efficient, making them ideal for audio applications. Class-B amplifiers improve efficiency by conducting in alternating halves but can introduce crossover distortion. Class-A/B combines both, offering a balance of efficiency and sound quality. Class-G and Class-H further enhance efficiency by varying the supply voltage, making them suitable for hi-fi applications. Class-D amplifiers, or digital amplifiers, utilize pulse-width modulation for high efficiency and compact design, popular in modern setups.</p></p>
