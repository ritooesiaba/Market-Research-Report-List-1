<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anti-graffiti Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sacrificial Coatings</li><li>Semi-sacrificial Coatings</li><li>Permanent Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-graffiti coatings market includes three main types: sacrificial coatings, semi-sacrificial coatings, and permanent coatings. Sacrificial coatings provide a temporary protective layer, allowing them to be easily removed along with graffiti, while semi-sacrificial coatings offer a balance, permitting some removal of graffiti with wear but still protecting the underlying surface. Permanent coatings create a durable barrier that resists graffiti adhesion and requires chemical solutions for removal. Each type serves specific needs based on durability, maintenance, and cost considerations.</p></p>
