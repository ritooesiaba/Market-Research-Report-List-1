<p><p>The sequencing reagents market encompasses three key types: Sanger sequencing, which is a traditional method utilizing chain-terminating inhibitors for DNA sequencing; Next Generation Sequencing (NGS), a high-throughput technology that enables rapid sequencing of multiple genes simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and scalability; and Third Generation Sequencing, which focuses on real-time, long-read sequencing techniques that allow for comprehensive analysis of complex genomes. Each method offers unique advantages and is utilized in various applications, including genomics, personalized medicine, and research.</p></p>
