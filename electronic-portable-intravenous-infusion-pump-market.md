<p><p>The electronic portable intravenous infusion pump market is categorized into two main types: Constant Volume Control Type and Syringe Injection Type. The Constant Volume Control Type allows precise regulation of fluid delivery, ensuring consistent medication dosing over time, which is crucial in critical care settings. In contrast, the Syringe Injection Type utilizes a syringe mechanism for administering medication, offering flexibility and convenience, especially in outpatient and home care. Both types enhance patient safety and treatment efficacy in intravenous therapy.</p></p>
