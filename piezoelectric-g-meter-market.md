<p><p>The piezoelectric G meter market is categorized based on the number of axes they measure: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis. A 1-axis G meter detects acceleration along a single direction, ideal for specific applications. The 2-axis G meter captures acceleration in two perpendicular directions, offering more comprehensive data for dynamic analysis. The 3-axis G meter measures acceleration in three dimensions, providing complete motion tracking and versatility, making it suitable for various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and vibrations testing.</p></p>
