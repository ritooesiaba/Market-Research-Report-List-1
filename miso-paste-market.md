<p><p>The miso paste market is categorized primarily into three types: white, red, and others. White miso, or shiro miso, is mild and sweeter, making it popular for soups and dressings. Red miso, or akamiso, has a stronger flavor and darker color, ideal for hearty dishes. The "others" category includes varieties like hatcho miso and flavored misos, catering to niche markets. Each type offers distinct taste profiles, serving diverse culinary applications and consumer preferences in various cuisines.</p></p>
