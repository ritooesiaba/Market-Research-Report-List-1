<p><p>The solar meter market comprises several types, including solar clamp meters, solar irradiance meters, solar power meters, and others. Solar clamp meters measure the current flowing through solar cables without disconnecting them, ensuring safe installation and maintenance. Solar irradiance meters assess solar radiation levels, helping optimize panel placement and performance. Solar power meters quantify the electrical output of solar systems, enabling efficiency monitoring. Additional types may include data loggers and testing devices, catering to diverse needs within the solar industry.</p></p>
