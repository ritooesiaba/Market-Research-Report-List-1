<p><p>The eSignature and Certifications market consists of three key types: SES (Simple Electronic Signature), AES (Advanced Electronic Signature), and QES (Qualified Electronic Signature). SES offers basic signature functionalities without stringent identity verification. AES provides a higher level of security and requires identity validation, ensuring the signer's authenticity. QES is the most robust, meeting strict legal standards and requiring a qualified certificate for signers, making it equivalent to a handwritten signature in legal contexts. Each type caters to different security and compliance needs.</p></p>
