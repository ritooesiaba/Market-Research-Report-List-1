<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasive Waterjet</li><li>Pure Waterjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting system market consists of two primary types: abrasive waterjet and pure waterjet. Abrasive waterjets incorporate abrasive materials into the high-pressure water stream, allowing for efficient cutting of hard materials like metals and stone. In contrast, pure waterjets utilize only high-pressure water, making them ideal for softer materials such as rubber and foam. Both systems offer precision cutting, minimal thermal distortion, and versatility across various industries, enhancing productivity and reducing waste in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
