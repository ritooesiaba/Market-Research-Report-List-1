<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photoresists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ARF Immersion Photoresist</li><li>ARF Dry Photoresist</li><li>KRF Photoresist</li><li>G-Line & I-Line Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist market is segmented into various types based on the wavelength of light used in lithography. ARF immersion photoresists are designed for advanced processes using 193 nm light in high-resolution applications. ARF dry photoresists, also at 193 nm, are utilized in standard photolithography. KRF photoresists operate at 248 nm and are primarily used in older technologies. G-Line and I-Line photoresists, operating at 436 nm and 365 nm respectively, serve legacy applications and less demanding processes. Each type caters to specific technology nodes and resolution requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
