<p><p>The roof hatch market features three primary types: galvanized steel, aluminum, and stainless steel. Galvanized steel roof hatches offer durability and corrosion resistance at a lower cost, making them suitable for industrial applications. Aluminum roof hatches are lightweight, resistant to rust, and ideal for environments where weight is a concern. Stainless steel roof hatches provide superior strength and corrosion resistance, making them preferable for harsh conditions or hygiene-sensitive areas, such as food processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
