<p><p>The water-based fire extinguisher market is primarily categorized into two types: portable and cart-mounted. Portable water-based extinguishers are lightweight and designed for easy transport, making them ideal for small fires in homes or offices. They typically contain a pressurized water tank and nozzle for direct application. In contrast, cart-type extinguishers are larger, mounted on wheels, and suitable for industrial or commercial settings. These units offer a higher volume of water, allowing for more extensive fire suppression capabilities in larger areas.</p></p>
