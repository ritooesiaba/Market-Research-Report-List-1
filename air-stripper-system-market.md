<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air stripper system market consists of two primary types: the sieve tray system and the packed tower system. The sieve tray system features multiple trays that facilitate gas-liquid contact, enhancing mass transfer for efficient volatile compound removal. In contrast, the packed tower system uses packing materials to create a large surface area for gas-liquid interaction, promoting effective stripping. Both systems are widely used in water treatment processes to eliminate impurities and VOCs, catering to various industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
