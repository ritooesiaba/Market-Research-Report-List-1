<p><strong>The Silicon Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Silicon Oil</li><li>Modified Silicon Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Straight Silicon Oil** market consists of pure, unmodified silicone fluids, primarily polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS), known for their thermal stability, water repellency, and lubricating properties. They are widely used in cosmetics, automotive, and industrial applications. The **Modified Silicon Oil** market includes silicone oils chemically altered to enhance specific properties, such as improved compatibility with organic materials, adhesion, or reactivity. These are tailored for specialized uses in coatings, adhesives, and personal care products, offering greater versatility compared to straight silicone oils.</p></p>
