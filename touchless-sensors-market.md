<p><p>The touchless sensors market comprises several types, including infrared sensors, which utilize light waves to detect movement without physical contact. Ultrasonic sound wave sensors emit sound waves and gauge distance based on reflected signals, allowing for non-contact operation. Electric or capacitive near-field sensors detect changes in capacitance when an object approaches, ensuring accurate readings without touch. Additionally, other types of touchless sensors may include laser-based and optical sensors, each enhancing user interaction and providing innovative solutions across various applications.</p></p>
