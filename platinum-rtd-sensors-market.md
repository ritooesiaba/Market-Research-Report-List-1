<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum RTD sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. Wire-Wound RTDs consist of a finely coiled platinum wire around a ceramic core, offering high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, Thin Film RTDs feature a thin layer of platinum deposited on a substrate, providing faster response times and enhanced durability. Both types serve industries such as aerospace, automotive, and HVAC, ensuring precise temperature measurement.</p></p>
