<p><p>River ferries primarily operate in two market types: Ro-Pax and Ro-Ro. Ro-Pax ferries transport both passengers and vehicles, accommodating a diverse range of travelers and facilitating seamless transfers between destinations. In contrast, Ro-Ro ferries focus on rolling cargo, allowing vehicles to drive on and off easily, which is essential for freight logistics. Both markets play crucial roles in enhancing connectivity and providing efficient transportation solutions within riverine ecosystems, catering to the needs of both passengers and commercial operators.</p></p>
