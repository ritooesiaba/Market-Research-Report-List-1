<p><p>The ceramic sputtering targets market is primarily classified into two types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets are flat and typically used in applications requiring a uniform coating over a sizable area, making them suitable for various electronics and optical devices. In contrast, rotary targets are cylindrical and designed for continuous deposition processes, increasing efficiency and reducing downtime in high-volume production settings. Both types serve essential roles in industries like semiconductor manufacturing, photovoltaics, and decorative coatings.</p></p>
