<p><p>The Teflon capacitor market is primarily segmented into two types: axial and radial Teflon capacitors. Axial Teflon capacitors feature leads on both ends, allowing for compact, space-efficient designs, making them ideal for applications where length is a constraint. In contrast, radial Teflon capacitors have leads that extend from the same side, facilitating easy mounting on circuit boards. Both types leverage Teflon's excellent dielectric properties, ensuring high performance in demanding environments, such as aerospace and high-frequency applications.</p></p>
