<p><p>Grain Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) is categorized into three main market types: Conventional, High Magnetic Strength, and Domain Refinement. The Conventional type offers standard performance with adequate magnetic properties for many applications. High Magnetic Strength GOES enhances efficiency and reduces energy losses, making it ideal for advanced electrical devices. Domain Refinement focuses on minimizing energy wastage by refining magnetic domains, resulting in superior magnetic performance. Together, these types cater to various industrial needs in electrical machines and transformers.</p></p>
