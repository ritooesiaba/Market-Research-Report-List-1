<p><p>The home electric vehicle (EV) charger market is primarily divided into two types: AC chargers and DC chargers. AC chargers, commonly Level 1 and Level 2, are suitable for overnight charging and are generally more affordable, using standard household outlets. In contrast, DC chargers provide rapid charging capabilities, significantly reducing charging time, making them ideal for those needing quick top-ups. While AC chargers dominate residential use due to cost and installation simplicity, DC chargers are often preferred for higher efficiency and performance.</p></p>
