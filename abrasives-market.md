<p><p>The abrasives market is categorized into three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasives grains bonded into a solid shape, commonly used in grinding wheels and cutting tools. Coated abrasives involve abrasive grains attached to a flexible backing, ideal for sanding and finishing applications. Super abrasives, made from advanced materials like diamond and CBN, are designed for high-performance grinding and cutting, offering superior durability and efficiency for precision tasks.</p></p>
