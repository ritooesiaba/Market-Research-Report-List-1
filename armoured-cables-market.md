<p><p>The Armoured Cables market is primarily categorized into two types: Aluminium Wire Armour (AWA) and Steel Wire Armour (SWA). AWA is lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for less demanding applications, offering a balance between robustness and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, SWA provides superior strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty environments and where higher mechanical protection is required. Both types enhance cable protection, ensuring safe transmission of electrical energy in diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
