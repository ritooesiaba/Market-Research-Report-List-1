<p><ul><li>Acid Antiscalants</li><li>Alkaline Antiscalants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RO membrane antiscalants are crucial for preventing scaling in reverse osmosis systems. Acid antiscalants, typically containing organic acids, are effective in mitigating calcium carbonate and sulfate scale, enhancing system efficiency. In contrast, alkaline antiscalants work to control scale formation by increasing pH, effectively managing silica and metal scales. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal RO membrane performance, extending lifespan, and reducing maintenance costs. Their application across various industries underlines their significance in water treatment processes.</p></p>
