<p><p>The handheld portable projectors market is categorized by technology, primarily including DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors for light processing, offering high brightness and contrast, while LCoS projectors provide superior color accuracy and smooth images. Additionally, projectors are differentiated by their physical resolution, such as 480p, 576p, 720p, and others, which indicate the clarity of the projected image. Higher resolutions provide better image quality, catering to varied consumer needs.</p></p>
