<p><p>The commercial vehicle drive shaft market comprises three primary types: aluminum, steel, and carbon fiber drive shafts. Aluminum drive shafts are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, enhancing fuel efficiency. Steel drive shafts offer durability and strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Carbon fiber drive shafts are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, providing superior performance and reduced weight, which improves vehicle handling.
