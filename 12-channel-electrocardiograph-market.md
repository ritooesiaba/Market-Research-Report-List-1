<p><strong>The 12 Channel Electrocardiograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 12-channel electrocardiograph market includes three primary types of ECG: Resting ECG, Stress ECG, and Holter ECG. Resting ECG devices assess heart activity during a stationary state, providing baseline cardiovascular information. Stress ECG equipment evaluates heart function under physical exertion, helping to identify issues that may not be apparent at rest. Holter ECG monitors heart rhythms over an extended period, typically 24 hours, capturing data during daily activities to detect irregularities that may occur sporadically.</p></p>
