<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underfloor heating mat market is primarily divided into two types: electric and water-based systems. Electric underfloor heating mats consist of heated coils or cables embedded in a mat, providing fast and efficient warmth, ideal for small to medium spaces. Water-based systems, on the other hand, circulate heated water through pipes laid beneath the floor, offering an even heat distribution and energy efficiency, suitable for larger areas. Both systems enhance comfort and energy efficiency in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
