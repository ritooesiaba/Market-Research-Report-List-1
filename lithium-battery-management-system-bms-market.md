<p><strong>The Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) monitors and manages individual cells in lithium-ion batteries, ensuring optimal performance, safety, and longevity. The market is categorized into Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS uses a single control unit to oversee all cells, simplifying design and cost but limiting scalability. In contrast, Distributed BMS employs multiple control units for individual cell groups, enhancing scalability and fault tolerance, making it suitable for larger and more complex battery systems, such as electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
