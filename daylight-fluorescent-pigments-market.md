<p><p>The Daylight Fluorescent Pigments market includes Thermoset Type, Thermoplastic Type, and Other (Aqueous Dispersions, etc.). Thermoset Type pigments are heat-resistant and used in coatings, plastics, and inks, curing irreversibly when heated. Thermoplastic Type pigments are melt-processable, ideal for applications requiring flexibility and recyclability, such as in plastics and textiles. Other types, like Aqueous Dispersions, are water-based, offering eco-friendly solutions for paints, inks, and coatings. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing durability, application versatility, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
