<p><p>The Reactive Diluents market is categorized into three types: aliphatic, aromatic, and cycloaliphatic. Aliphatic reactive diluents are linear hydrocarbons that enhance flexibility and low viscosity in formulations. Aromatic reactive diluents, containing benzene rings, offer superior reactivity and stability, suitable for high-performance applications. Cycloaliphatic reactive diluents combine characteristics of both aliphatic and aromatic types, providing moderate viscosity with improved thermal stability. Each type serves specific applications in coatings, adhesives, and composites, influencing performance and characteristics.</p></p>
