<p><p>The Portable HbA1c Analyzers market features three primary testing methods: Ion-exchange HPLC, Immunoassay, and Boronate Affinity Technology. Ion-exchange HPLC efficiently separates hemoglobin variants, providing accurate HbA1c levels. Immunoassay methods utilize antibodies to detect and quantify HbA1c, offering rapid results. Meanwhile, Boronate Affinity Technology leverages boronic acid's interaction with glycation sites on hemoglobin for precise HbA1c measurement. Each technique caters to the growing demand for convenient, quick, and reliable blood glucose monitoring solutions in clinical settings.</p></p>
