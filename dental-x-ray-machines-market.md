<p><p>The dental X-ray machines market comprises various types, including ordinary X-rays and panoramic X-rays. Ordinary X-rays are standard imaging tools used for diagnosing cavities, tooth decay, and bone issues, providing clear, localized views of specific dental areas. On the other hand, panoramic X-rays capture a broad, 2D image of the entire dental arch, including teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures, aiding in comprehensive assessments and treatment planning. Both types are essential for effective dental diagnostics and patient care.</p></p>
