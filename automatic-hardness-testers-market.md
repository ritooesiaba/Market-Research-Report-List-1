<p><ul><li>Rockwell Hardness Tester</li><li>Brinell Hardness Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Hardness Testers market includes various types, primarily Rockwell and Brinell hardness testers. Rockwell hardness testers measure material hardness by applying a specific load using a steel ball or diamond indenter, providing quick results on various materials. In contrast, Brinell hardness testers use a larger steel ball indenter to create an impression and measure the diameter of the indentation, making it suitable for materials with coarser structures. Both methods are essential for quality control in manufacturing and materials science.</p></p>
