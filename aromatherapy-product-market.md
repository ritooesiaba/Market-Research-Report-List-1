<p><p>Aromatherapy products are primarily categorized into three market types: topical application, aerial diffusion, and direct inhalation. Topical application involves the use of essential oils diluted in carrier oils for skin absorption, promoting relaxation or healing. Aerial diffusion encompasses the dispersal of essential oils into the air using diffusers, enhancing ambiance and mood. Direct inhalation involves smelling essential oils directly from the bottle or using inhalers, providing immediate benefits for stress relief and respiratory health, making each method uniquely effective.</p></p>
