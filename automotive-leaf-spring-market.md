<p><ul><li>Double End</li><li>Open End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive leaf spring market features two primary types: double end and open end leaf springs. Double end leaf springs have eyes or loops at both ends, allowing for secure attachment and better load distribution, often used in heavier vehicles. In contrast, open end leaf springs have a terminal at one end, enabling customization in design and flexible fitting options in various applications. Both types are crucial for vehicle suspension systems, enhancing stability and load-carrying capacity.</p></p>
