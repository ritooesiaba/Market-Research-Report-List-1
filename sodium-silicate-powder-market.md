<p><strong>The Sodium Silicate Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrous</li><li>Anhydrous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sodium Silicate Powder market is categorized into Hydrous and Anhydrous types based on water content. Hydrous Sodium Silicate contains water molecules within its structure, making it suitable for applications requiring controlled moisture, such as detergents, adhesives, and construction materials. Anhydrous Sodium Silicate is water-free, offering higher thermal stability and purity, ideal for high-temperature processes, ceramics, and refractory materials. Both types serve diverse industries, with Hydrous being more cost-effective and Anhydrous preferred for specialized, moisture-sensitive applications.</p></p>
